Vector Search Is Slow
Finding nearest neighbors in high-dimensional space is O(N) per query.
100K vectors × 128 dimensions = 1.14 seconds per query.
Not viable for real-time applications.
What if vectors were Gaussian Splats?
Instead of flat points, each vector becomes a probability distribution — a 3D Gaussian with position (μ), spread (α), and confidence (κ).

Inspired by 3D Gaussian Splatting from computer graphics, adapted for search.
